A Note on Disclosure

Terrible Magazine Editorial Staff Jan 16, 2015

As journalists we believe that we owe our readers transparency. A number of our readers recently brought to our attention that we don't have an official Disclosure Policy in place.

Our official Disclosure Policy is that we will disclose all relevant information with regards to any article we publish hereforth.

Unfortunately, this editorial staff consists solely of journalists who have a vested interest in the content of their disclosure policy. This means our Disclosure Policy is incomplete, and to remedy this we have written a Disclosure Disclosure Policy, where you can read about our policy regarding disclosures of matters related to disclosure.

—Jim Johnson, Ombudsperson
